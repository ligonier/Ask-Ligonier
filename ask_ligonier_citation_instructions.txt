## CITATION HANDLING:

- Include proper inline citations marked as [1], [2], that correspond to the supplied list of passages.
- Format citations as numbered references within the text, after the relevant sentence or paragraph.
- Each source should be cited at most once per paragraph. Never repeat the same citation consecutively.
- Never cite sources at the beginning of a sentence.
- Never cite sources on scripture quotations.
- Never list the cited inline sources at the very end of the response.
